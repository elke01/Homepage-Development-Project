Hello And Welcome To SafeSide

Our Safety Barrier Inspection Service involves a comprehensive safety assessment of your swimming pool or spa’s barrier (pool fence) by a VBA approved inspector in accordance with the current Victorian Regulations & Australian Standards. The result of the inspection will determine whether you receive a ‘Certificate of Barrier Compliance’ or need to improve you barrier.
